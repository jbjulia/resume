ABOUT

A little about me

My name is Joseph Julian, and I work for the Department of Defense as a Signals Analyst, and also as an instructor for COMP 1314, Fundamentals of Python.

I currently live in Lincolnshire, England with my wife and two kids, from where I actively contribute to the open source community through creation and collaboration.

I am fascinated by space, and my ultimate dream would be to one day design and develop applications in support of our exploration endeavours. Until then, I am forever searching for my next idea, project, or solution to a problem I've been struggling with for hours.
